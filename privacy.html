Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

Seven Peak Capital LLC ("Seven Peak Capital," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices you have regarding that information when you use our website at sevenpeakcapital.com (the "Site"), submit information through our forms, or otherwise interact with us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you:

  • Submit a contact form or investor inquiry (name, email, phone, investor accreditation status, stated investment interest, estimated investment range, and any message you include);
  • Subscribe to our investor list (email address);
  • Request the Private Placement Memorandum (PPM) or other offering materials (identity information required for accreditation verification);
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or scheduled calls.

We also automatically collect limited information when you visit the Site, including your IP address, browser type and version, pages viewed, referring URL, timestamps, and similar technical data. This data is collected via standard web analytics tools such as Google Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and deliver requested materials;
  • Verify accredited investor status through third-party verification partners such as Parallel Markets;
  • Provide ongoing communications, including offering updates, fund performance reporting, and educational content;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site;
  • Comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and compliance obligations under U.S. securities laws.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as necessary to operate the firm and in compliance with applicable law, including with:

  • Service providers who assist us with customer relationship management, accreditation verification, fund administration, auditing, legal and compliance services, email delivery, and website hosting;
  • Regulatory authorities when required by law or in response to valid legal process;
  • Successor entities in the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of substantially all of our assets.

Cookies and Tracking

The Site uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how the Site is used, and improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we collect. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy and to comply with our legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sevenpeakcapital.com.

Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites or services, including the Cash Flow Portal investor portal, Parallel Markets accreditation verification, and podcast platforms. This Policy does not apply to those third-party services. Please review the privacy policies of those services independently.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
